Research & Publications

Contributing to the advancement of materials science and nanotechnology.

POSTER

Synthesis of Zn-Doped SrO Nanoparticles

ICEI 2026 | Materials Science Track | Co-Author

Controlled doping (0.1% and 0.5% Zn) at 120-360°C. Full characterization: XRD, SEM, TEM, FTIR, DLS, UV-Vis. Band gap reduction: 3.237 eV → 2.287 eV.

Investigation of SrO for Water Splitting

ICEI 2026 | Nanotechnology Track | Co-Author

Pure SrO via hydrothermal method. Band gap variation: 3.71 eV → 3.055 eV. Evaluated for photoelectric and water-splitting applications.
